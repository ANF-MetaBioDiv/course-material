ANF MetaBioDiv

Third edition (September 2025)

Welcome to the third edition of the ANF Exploration de la Diversité Taxonomique des Ecosystèmes par Metabarcoding. The aim of this course is to teach you how to run microbial ecology analyses (mostly alpha and beta diversity) from metabarcoding raw sequencing data using R.
