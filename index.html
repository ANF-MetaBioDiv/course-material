ANF MetaBioDiv

First edition (September 2022)

Welcome to the first edition of the ANF Exploration de la Diversité Taxonomique des Ecosystèmes par Metabarcoding. The aim of this course is to teach you how to run microbial ecology analyses (mostly alpha and beta diversity) from metabarcoding raw sequencing data using R.
